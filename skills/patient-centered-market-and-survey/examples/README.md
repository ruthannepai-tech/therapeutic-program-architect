# Worked example — Eosinophilic esophagitis (EoE)

These files are the real deliverables the skill's templates and helpers were
distilled from — a pMHC/antigen-specific tolerance-vaccine program for EoE.

| File | Maps to |
|---|---|
| `eoe_market_context.md` | Market report §1 (disease/epidemiology, comparative snapshot) |
| `eoe_market_analysis.md` | Market report §2–4, §9 (sizing, competitive whitespace, business model, assumptions) |
| `eoe_survey_design_plan.md` | Survey blueprint (principles, research questions, sampling, domains, instrument anchors, analysis, ethics) |
| `eoe_survey_distribution_kit.md` | Survey distribution kit (live-form path, recruitment copy) |

The `kernel.py` helpers reproduce this example's headline numbers exactly:
US EoE at 1/2000, 60% diagnosed, 55% eligible, 15% mature SOM →
~$2.2B TAM, ~$1.2B SOM at a $150k one-course price, ~3.8-yr payback vs. a
$40k/yr chronic biologic, ~$250k/patient saved by year 10.

Figures are assumption-driven planning estimates, not guidance; the survey
instrument is a pre-IRB draft.
